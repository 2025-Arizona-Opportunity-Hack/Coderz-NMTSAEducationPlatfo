About Neurologic Music Therapy

Neurologic Music Therapy (NMT) is a research-based treatment system that uses music and rhythm to improve brain function. For individuals with autism, NMT can help develop:

  • Motor skills and body awareness through rhythm-based movement
  • Speech, language, and communication abilities
  • Social interaction and emotional regulation
  • Independence and meaningful connections with others
