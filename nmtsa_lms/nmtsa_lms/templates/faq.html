{% trans "General Questions" %}

{% trans "What is NMTSA Learning?" %}
{% trans "NMTSA Learning is an online Learning Management System (LMS) dedicated to neurologic music therapy education. We provide comprehensive courses for healthcare professionals, therapists, educators, and families with special needs members. Our platform offers video lessons, interactive content, and AI-powered support to enhance your learning experience." %}
{% trans "Who can use NMTSA Learning?" %}
{% blocktrans %}Our platform serves two primary audiences:
  • Healthcare Professionals & Therapists: Music therapists, occupational therapists, speech therapists, educators, and other professionals seeking continuing education and certification.
  • Families & Caregivers: Parents, guardians, and caregivers of individuals with special needs who want to learn therapeutic techniques and strategies.
You must be at least 18 years old to create an account.{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Is NMTSA Learning free?" %}
{% trans "NMTSA Learning offers both free and paid courses. You can browse our course catalog without an account. Some introductory courses are free, while specialized and certification courses require a one-time purchase. Prices vary by course complexity and content. Once purchased, you have lifetime access to the course materials." %}

{% trans "Account & Registration" %}

{% trans "How do I create an account?" %}
{% blocktrans %}Creating an account is easy:
  1. Click the "Login" button in the top navigation
  2. Select "Sign up" on the Auth0 login page
  3. Enter your email and create a password (or use social login)
  4. Select your role: Student/Family or Educator/Therapist
  5. Complete the onboarding process
Teachers must upload credentials for admin verification before creating courses.{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "I forgot my password. How do I reset it?" %}
{% blocktrans %}To reset your password:
  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Follow the instructions to create a new password
If you don't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder.{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Can I delete my account?" %}
{% blocktrans %}Yes, you can delete your account at any time from your account settings. Please note that account deletion is permanent and will remove:
  • Your profile and personal information
  • Course progress and completion data
  • Certificates and achievements
  • Access to purchased courses
Note: Account deletion does not automatically issue refunds. For refund inquiries, contact support before deleting your account.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Courses & Enrollment" %}

{% trans "How do I enroll in a course?" %}
{% blocktrans %}To enroll in a course:
  1. Browse courses at /courses/
  2. Click on a course to view details
  3. Click "Enroll" (for free courses) or "Purchase" (for paid courses)
  4. Complete payment via PayPal (if applicable)
  5. Access the course from your student dashboard
{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "How long do I have access to a course?" %}
{% blocktrans %}Once you enroll in a course (free or paid), you have lifetime access. You can:
  • Complete the course at your own pace
  • Re-watch videos as many times as needed
  • Download course materials (if provided)
  • Access updated content when teachers improve the course
Your progress is automatically saved and synced across devices.{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Can I get a certificate after completing a course?" %}
{% blocktrans %}Yes! Upon completing all lessons and assignments in a course, you'll receive a certificate of completion. Certificates include:
  • Your name and course title
  • Completion date
  • Teacher's signature
  • Unique verification code
You can download certificates as PDF files and share them on LinkedIn or with employers.{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "What if I'm not satisfied with a course?" %}
{% blocktrans %}We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. You can request a full refund if:
  • You request the refund within 30 days of purchase
  • You've completed less than 25% of the course content
To request a refund, contact support@nmtsalearning.org with your order details.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "For Teachers" %}

{% trans "How do I become a teacher on NMTSA Learning?" %}
{% blocktrans %}To become a teacher:
  1. Sign up and select "Educator/Therapist" as your role
  2. Complete the teacher onboarding process
  3. Upload your professional credentials (degree, licenses, certifications)
  4. Wait for admin verification (typically 2-5 business days)
  5. Once approved, you can create and publish courses
{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "How do I create a course?" %}
{% blocktrans %}After teacher verification:
  1. Go to your Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click "Create New Course"
  3. Add course details (title, description, thumbnail)
  4. Create modules and lessons (videos, blog posts, quizzes)
  5. Submit course for admin review
  6. Once approved, publish your course
Note: All courses must be reviewed by admins before going live.{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Can I earn money from my courses?" %}
{% blocktrans %}Yes! Teachers can set prices for their courses. You choose whether to offer free or paid courses. Revenue is distributed as follows:
  • Teacher Share: 70% of course revenue
  • Platform Fee: 30% (covers hosting, support, and infrastructure)
Payouts are processed monthly via PayPal. You can track earnings in your Teacher Dashboard.{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Technical Support" %}

{% trans "What browsers are supported?" %}
{% blocktrans %}NMTSA Learning works best on modern browsers:
  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari (macOS/iOS)
  • Microsoft Edge
We recommend using the latest version of your browser for optimal performance and security.{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Videos won't play. What should I do?" %}
{% blocktrans %}If videos aren't playing, try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  4. Try a different browser
  5. Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers)
  6. Update your browser to the latest version
If issues persist, contact support@nmtsalearning.org with details.{% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Is NMTSA Learning accessible?" %}
{% blocktrans %}Yes! We're committed to accessibility and follow WCAG 2.1 AAA guidelines:
  • Autism-Friendly Design: Zero animations, clear navigation, predictable layouts
  • Theme Options: Light, Dark, High Contrast, and Minimal modes
  • Font Controls: Adjustable text size (Small, Medium, Large)
  • Keyboard Navigation: Full keyboard accessibility
  • Screen Readers: ARIA labels and semantic HTML
  • Video Captions: Transcripts provided for all video content
{% endblocktrans %}
